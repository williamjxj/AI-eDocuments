### Problem Statement & Opportunity

Enterprises rely on large volumes of documents to support critical business operations. In parallel, they accumulate vast repositories of legacy documents that must be assessed for reuse, integration, compliance, or retirement. Managing, maintaining, integrating, archiving, and extracting value from these documents remains a persistent and costly challenge.

At the same time, enterprises are increasingly seeking to apply AI technologies—such as **RAG, GraphRAG, and LoRA**—to document-centric workflows to improve search, knowledge extraction, automation, and decision-making. Demand for these capabilities is growing rapidly, creating a strong and expanding market opportunity.

As the founder of **Best IT Consultants**, I plan to focus on this space by building practical, cost-effective AI-powered document solutions and offering them as products and services to enterprise customers.
